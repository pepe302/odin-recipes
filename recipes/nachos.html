Super Nachos

These homemade nachos are great for game day. This recipe makes a huge meal-sized tray of nachos loaded with taco-seasoned ground beef, refried beans, salsa, jalapeños, and cheese.

Ingredients:

  • Nacho
  • Cheese
  • Green Onions
  • Jalapeños

Steps:

  1. Spread the tortilla chips on a baking sheet.
  2. Top the chips with cheese, beans.
  3. Broil until the cheese is melted.
  4. Top with olives, salsa, sour cream, green onions, and jalapeños.
